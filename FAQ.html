Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SkyCortex OS, how to navigate the portfolio, discover its interactive experiences, and explore every creative project.

What is SkyCortex OS?

SkyCortex OS is a creative digital laboratory that combines technology, storytelling, illustration, UX design, programming, and interactive experiences into one evolving portfolio where ideas become real projects.

What can I explore here?

SkyCortex OS is organized into several creative sections:

  • Play – Interactive tarot experiences and creative games.
  • Read – Original manga and storytelling projects.
  • Build – Programming experiments, interactive applications, and creative coding.
  • Create – Handmade macramé and artistic creations.
  • What's New – The latest projects and recent updates.
  • About – Learn more about the creator and the story behind SkyCortex OS.
  • Let's Work Together – Contact information and collaboration opportunities.

What is the Tarot Game?

The Tarot Game is an original reading experience inspired by the Japanese Maneki-neko lucky cat. It is currently available as a beta version while new features, tools, and reading systems continue to be developed.

How do I read the manga?

Every illustration on the homepage can be clicked or tapped to view it in full size. To explore the complete story, simply select Read the Original Manga.

Who created SkyCortex OS?

SkyCortex OS was created and developed by Gloria Martinez in Costa Rica. It combines front-end development, UX design, creative coding, digital art, illustration, storytelling, and interactive experiences into one evolving platform.

How can I contact you?

Visit the Let's Work Together page to learn how to get in touch. Whether you have a question, a freelance opportunity, or a creative collaboration in mind, I'd be happy to hear from you.

Can I use the artwork, illustrations, or code?

Unless otherwise stated, all illustrations, original stories, designs, concepts, branding, and creative content featured on SkyCortex OS are the intellectual property of the creator. Please do not reproduce or distribute this material without permission.

Where was SkyCortex OS created?

SkyCortex OS is an independent creative project proudly designed and developed in Costa Rica.

Absolutely. New projects, coding experiments, illustrations, manga chapters, handmade art, UX improvements, and interactive experiences are continuously being added. Every visit may reveal something new.
