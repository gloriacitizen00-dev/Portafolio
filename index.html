About me

Soy Gloria Martínez. This site is my space to share ideas in pre‑launch, invite people to try my games before they’re released, and show how digital science can blend with creativity. The first game I’m building works like a deep learning training: it tries to memorize images, avoid repeating patterns, and learn the meaning of each card to give a clearer, more accurate reading. What’s coming excites me even more: new features that get closer to the real experience of reading cards, bringing together technology and intuition. All the designs you see here are part of my personal creation and belong entirely to me.
