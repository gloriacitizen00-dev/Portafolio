About SkyCortex OS

```html id="y7r4mk"

SkyCortex OS is an evolving digital space built around creativity, technology, learning, and human connection.

What started as a personal idea slowly became a growing ecosystem where art, experimentation, and software development come together. More than a portfolio, it is a place to explore new ideas, create meaningful projects, and share the process of learning and building.

My goal is to create a modern digital environment where technology feels creative, expressive, and accessible — not distant. I believe software can be both functional and artistic, combining structure, design, and imagination into experiences that people can genuinely connect with.

As a junior Full Stack Java Developer, I continue learning every day while building web applications, interactive systems, and creative experiments using technologies like Java, Spring Boot, React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and PostgreSQL.

More than anything, SkyCortex OS is about growth, curiosity, and community — a space where people can create, collaborate, and evolve together through technology and creativity.
```
