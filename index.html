About the Creator of SkyCortex OS

Hi, I'm Gloria.

I am a Front-End Developer, UX Designer, and Creative Technologist who enjoys sketching ideas, designing immersive interfaces, and transforming imagination into digital experiences that are both functional and engaging.

I created SkyCortex OS because I wanted a place that could grow alongside me—not just as a portfolio, but as a living collection of everything I build, learn, and imagine.

Every project here represents a new challenge. Some began as small experiments, while others evolved into complete applications, websites, creative stories, or design concepts. Together, they reflect my passion for technology, creativity, and continuous learning.

My work combines responsive web development, user experience design, SEO, and creative problem-solving. I enjoy creating digital products that are both functional and visually engaging, always with the goal of making technology feel intuitive and accessible.

SkyCortex OS is more than a showcase of finished work. It's an evolving workspace where ideas become real projects, skills grow through practice, and curiosity drives every new challenge.

Whether you're here as a recruiter, collaborator, or simply exploring, I hope this space gives you a clear picture of who I am, how I think, and the kind of digital experiences I strive to create.
