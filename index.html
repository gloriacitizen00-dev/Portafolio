About me

🚀 This site is my little launchpad to drop ideas in pre‑launch, let people try my games before they’re out, and show how digital science + creativity can actually vibe together.

🧠 The first game I’m building is like a deep learning workout: it tries to memorize images, avoid repeating patterns, and learn the meaning of each card so the readings feel sharper and more real.

🔮 And honestly, what’s coming next excites me even more: new features that get closer to the real experience of reading cards, mixing technology with intuition in ways that feel alive.

🎨 Every design here is my own creation and belongs entirely to me.

🌱 Plus, I care about preserving nature—because innovation should respect the world we live in.
