About the Creator of SkyCortex OS

Hi, I'm Gloria.

I am a technology enthusiast, Front-End Developer, and UX Designer who enjoys exploring the intersection between creativity and technology. I love learning new tools, experimenting with code, designing interfaces, creating illustrations, and transforming ideas into interactive digital experiences.

From sketching concepts on paper to spending hours exploring nature and finding inspiration in the world around me, I believe creativity has no limits. Through programming, storytelling, and design, I enjoy building experiences that go beyond expectations and bring imagination into the digital world.

SkyCortex OS started as a university portfolio project, but over time it evolved into something much bigger: a living digital laboratory where I document my journey, experiment with new technologies, and transform ideas into real projects.

My vision is to continue growing SkyCortex into a creative learning space where technology, design, storytelling, and curiosity come together to inspire others to explore, create, and discover what they can build.

Every project inside SkyCortex represents a challenge, a lesson, and a new adventure. Whether it is web development, UX design, sketching, digital art, or interactive storytelling, my goal is always the same: create experiences that feel meaningful, intuitive, and memorable.
